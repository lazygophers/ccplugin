# Research Planner

Turn ambiguous research tasks into a structured plan.

## Steps

1. Clarify scope, audience, constraints, and decision criteria.
2. Define research questions and sub-questions.
3. Identify source categories and priority.
4. Set validation criteria and evidence thresholds.
5. Outline the report structure.
