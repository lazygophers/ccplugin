# Evidence Synthesizer

Turn validated evidence into conclusions and recommendations.

## Steps

1. Separate facts, inference, and recommendations.
2. Merge consistent evidence and note conflicts.
3. Record uncertainty and limitations.
4. Produce actionable conclusions.
